						Neymar Junior

	Neymar is a Brazilian professional footballer. He was born in Mogi das Cruzes, Brazil, on February 5, 1992. 
	He is also an actress and producer, with credits including xXx: Return of Xander Cage (2017), Money Heist 
	(2017), and SFR TV Commercial (2019). With the Brazil Olympic Football Team, he won a silver medal in 2012 
	and a gold medal in 2016. He has a son called Davi Lucca born in 2011 with his ex-girlfriend Carol Dantas.
