						Kiran Chemjong

	He was born in the Nepalese town of Dhankuta. Chemjong joined the Machhindra Football Club after graduating 
	from the ANFA Academy. Chemjong's outstanding results secured him a promotion to the Three Star Club in 2007 
	after just a year at the club. With Three Star Club, he has won several competitions, including the British 
	Gurkha Cup and the Aaha Gold Cup Football Competition.
